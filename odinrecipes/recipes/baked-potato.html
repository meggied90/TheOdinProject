A Basic Baked Potato

Ingredients

  • 1 potato per person
  • Salt and pepper
  • Butter (1 tbsp per person)
  • Optional: sour cream
  • Optional: green onions / chives
  • Optional: bacon crumbles
  • Optional: cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees
  2. Stab each potato several times with a pronged fork or thin pointy metal object. This prevents the potato from EXPLODING.
  3. Wrap the potato in tin foil and bake for 1 hour. If they are very large potatoes they may need 1.5 hours.
  4. Remove potato from oven.
  5. Once it is cool enough to handle, slice it open and add the butter, salt, and pepper to taste. Add any additional toppings you like as well.
